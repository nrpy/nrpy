## Scope

This page records which raw sources and source aggregates feed durable wiki pages. It follows the LLM Wiki raw-source principle from Karpathy's `LLM Wiki`: raw sources remain the source of truth, while the wiki is maintained as a compiled layer between users and those sources.

Use this map to route source drift and partial-ingest follow-up. Do not treat it as a complete reverse dependency database yet.

Authority tiers follow [Schema](SCHEMA.md): `primary-code`, `primary-test`, `generated-evidence`, `primary-doc`, `external-spec`, and `background`.

Claim-evidence-block coverage follows the Schema's prospective adoption
boundary. The 07-13-2026 adoption change asserts no completed block coverage;
claims changed in that change remain baseline-uncovered unless their exact block
is present. Migrate a baseline claim when it or its deciding source is next
materially changed after adoption.
